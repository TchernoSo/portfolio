About ME


I began my self-taught journey over 15 years ago, starting from my passion for football and expanding into art and programming.

When I was younger, I dreamed of becoming a professional football player, putting a lot of effort into improving my fitness and learning about various strategies and techniques. At the age of 12, I signed with my first amateur club where just 5 years later I experienced an ACL knee injury that required surgery and nearly a year of recovery, resulting in a loss of confidence and strength.
... Three years later, I discovered my second passion in the world of art: dance. That changed my life completely, I left home, family, and friends to relocate to London, a city known for its vibrant art scene, creativity, and diversity.

In 2020, the pandemic had a significant impact on the world. During this time of isolation, I faced numerous challenges that released deeper aspects of life, resulting in an upgrade of the best version of myself. Ever since I've been sharing tips and techniques to motivate others to overcome life's challenges and achieve their best. (TS365)

I believe that my skills, knowledge, and experience can offer hope and purpose to those willing to aim for improvement in their lives.
