About ME

I am self-taught in diverse domains including art, sports, and programming. My journey of constant growth and exploration started over 15 years ago with my childhood passion for football. I studied strategies and techniques to improve my knowledge and skills, eventually signing with my first amateur club at age 12. Years later, following ACL knee surgery, I traveled to another country to immerse myself in the world of music and dance for over four years and since 2022, I have been dedicated to mastering computer science and programming.

In 2020, I experienced unparalleled difficulties that resulted in a period of isolation, but also provided an opportunity for personal growth and realization. Since then, I have been actively developing and providing tips, techniques, and inspiration to help motivate others around the world on their journeys of learning, growing, and becoming the best versions of themselves. (TS365)

My goal is to use what I went through during that time to awaken hope and meaning for anyone ready to level up, just like I did.
