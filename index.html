Sport

96's

At the age of two, I moved from the Netherlands to Portugal, with a deep passion for football.
I never left home without a ball, as it was the only thing I brought from home.
Growing up in Portugal, spending hours kicking the ball against the wall and playing alone in the streets, I eventually made amazing friends and joined my first amateur football club.

Just four years later, I experienced an ACL injury, which required surgery and six to eight months of recovery. This led to a decline in my confidence and fitness, prompting me to take a break. (I never imagined I would come back!)

Art

2k16

Honestly, if jobs didn't exist, we would all end up becoming artists. We all have an artistic side, but only a few can express it and follow it. The purpose of a human being is to positively impact the world every single day, and Art is one of our abilities to make this happen.

Growing up in an environment of music and dance every day, later I decided to pursue the art of dancing.

My life once again changed after leaving another home, quitting my job, selling all my belongings, and relocating to London, a city known for its vibrant art scene, creativity, and diversity, and the rest... is History!
