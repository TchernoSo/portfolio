About ME


I began my self-taught journey over 15 years ago, starting from my passion for football and later expanding into art, coaching and programming.

When I was younger, I dreamed of becoming a professional football player, putting a lot of effort into improving my fitness and technique.
At the age of 12, I signed with my first amateur club, but just 5 years later I experienced a ACL knee injury that required surgery and nearly a year of recovery. This resulted in a loss of confidence and strength.
...
Three years later, I discovered my second passion: Dance. That changed my life forever, leaving home, family, and friends behind to relocate to London, a city known for its vibrant art scene, creativity, and diversity to expand my knowledge in Art.

In 2020, the pandemic had a significant impact on the world. During isolation, I faced challenges that revealed deeper aspects of life, leading to personal growth. Since then, I have been empowering others to overcome challenges and strive towards their best selves.(TS365)

My name is Tcherno and this is my message to you "Our journey is not just about the time between birth and death, it's about the legacy we leave behind."
