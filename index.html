Born in the Netherlands, moved to Portugal at the age of two.
My mother remembers how I always carried the ball with me, refusing to leave it behind, as it was the only thing I brought from home.
I would spend hours kicking the ball against the wall and playing alone in the streets until I started to be surrounded by amazing friends, and a few years later I joined my first amateur football club.
Just 4 years later, I suffered an ACL injury, leading to surgery and six to eight months of recovery. This resulted in a decrease of my confidence and fitness, so I decided to take a break. (Never knew I'd come back!)
