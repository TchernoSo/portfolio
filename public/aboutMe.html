Sport

00's

I moved from the Netherlands to Portugal when I was two, with a big passion for football.
Growing up in Portugal was a challenge, I would spend hours in the street kicking the ball against the wall on my own, until I started making friends and later ended up joining my first amateur football club.

Unfortunately, five years later, I suffered an ACL injury that required surgery and a recovery period of 9 months. I never thought I would have to take such a long break, but the lack of confidence, strength, and circumstances forced me to do so.

Art

2k16

New passion unlocked.
The most unexpected scene in my life was when I decided to pursue the art of dance. It became a part of me. Whenever a track reached my ears, I would find myself dancing at any moment of the day. After 7 months in this scenario, I finally decided to level up my skills and knowledge about art.

My life once again changed after leaving another home, quitting my job, selling all my belongings, and relocating to London, a city known for its vibrant art scene, creativity, and diversity, and the rest... is History!
